Ceremonial matcha from Japan. Crafted with care. A calm, refined energy.

Premium matcha for clean energy

At Matcha Energy, quality is our first priority. We use EU-certified organic matcha sourced from Japan, made from ceremonial grade, first-harvest leaves.

Matcha is a healthier alternative to coffee, providing smooth and steady energy without the crash. Unlike coffee, it is generally lower in acidity, making it gentler on the stomach and less likely to cause irritation or discomfort. The natural combination of caffeine and L-theanine in matcha supports calm focus, helping reduce jitters and sharp spikes in energy. Matcha is also rich in antioxidants, which support overall wellbeing and make it a more balanced daily choice compared to many coffee-based drinks.

We prepare our own syrups using real fruits and never use artificial colors. All colors come naturally from matcha and fruits.

Our snacks are baked fresh daily in small quantities, and all stock is cleared by the end of the day.
